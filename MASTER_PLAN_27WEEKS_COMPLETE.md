## Training Principles

### The 10% Rule (Non-Negotiable)
- Weekly mileage increases max 10% per week
- "Feeling good" = yellow flag, not green light
- Bank miles Tuesday = steal from Thursday workout quality

### Deload Weeks (Strategic Recovery)
- Drop to 70% of previous week's volume
- Maintain vert where possible (Tuesday group run)
- Keep workout intensity, reduce volume
- Non-negotiable even if feeling strong
- Foundation: Weeks 4, 8
- Durability: Weeks 13, 17, 20
- Specificity: Week 24

### Front-Loading Warning
- Your aerobic system recovers faster than tissues
- 8+ weeks needed for musculoskeletal adaptation
- Starting from 12mpw requires patience

### Shuffle Running Strategy
- Foundation Phase: Walk breaks encouraged on >6% grades
- Durability Phase: Progressive continuous uphill work
  - Week 18: 500ft continuous
  - Week 19: 750ft continuous
  - Week 20: 1000ft continuous (test before deload)
- Specificity Phase: Race-specific continuous climbs
- Supported by daily PT exercises (calf/hip flexor endurance)

### Weekly Structure
- **Monday:** Rest + PT Foundation (Weeks 1-9 only)
- **Tuesday:** Group run (6-8mi, 1500ft) + Heavy strength + PT (Weeks 1-9)
- **Wednesday:** Easy run + PT (Weeks 1-9)
- **Thursday:** Quality workout + Heavy strength + PT (Weeks 1-9)
- **Friday:** Easy run OR rowing + PT (Weeks 1-9)
- **Saturday:** Rest + PT (Weeks 1-9)
- **Sunday:** Long run + PT (Weeks 1-9)

### Strength Training
- **PT Foundation (Weeks 1-9):** **DAILY comprehensive PT (20-25min)** + heavy 2x/week
- **Runner Maintenance (Weeks 10-27):** Heavy 2x/week + maintenance 3x/week (10-15min)
- **Race Week (Aug 10-15):** Maintenance only

# COMPREHENSIVE PT FOUNDATION PROGRAM (Weeks 1-9)

## Daily PT Routine (20-25 minutes)
**Perform ALL exercises daily throughout Foundation Phase (63 consecutive days)**

### Hip Flexor Strengthening
1. **Seated Banded Hip Flexor March**
   - 3x15-20 each leg
   - Band around foot, lift knee against resistance
   - Focus on controlled eccentric lowering

2. **Psoas Plank**
   - 3x30sec
   - Forearm plank position, lift one foot 1-2" off ground
   - Alternate legs each set

3. **Hip Marches**
   - 2x15 each leg
   - Standing, drive knee to 90°, hold 1 sec at top

### Adductor Strengthening (⚠️ Key weakness area)
1. **Side Plank Clam Raise**
   - 2x15 each side
   - Side plank position with bent knees
   - Lift top knee while maintaining plank

2. **Side-Lying Adduction** (‼️ Priority)
   - 3x15 each leg
   - Bottom leg lifts against gravity
   - Optional: Add ankle weight when achieving 3x15 easily

3. **Copenhagen Plank** (‼️ Priority)
   - 3x20sec each side
   - Progress to 3x30sec by Week 6
   - Top leg on bench/chair, bottom leg hovering

### Abductor Maintenance
1. **Monster Walks**
   - 3 sets of 18ft (one direction)
   - Band around ankles or just above knees
   - Maintain tension throughout

2. **Side Walks with Band**
   - 3 sets of 9ft each direction
   - Athletic stance, controlled steps

3. **Fonda** (Fire hydrants)
   - 3x15 each side
   - On hands and knees, lift bent leg to side
   - Control through full ROM

4. **Single-Leg Balance Eyes Closed**
   - 3x30sec each leg
   - Progress to unstable surface (foam pad) by Week 7

### Glutes & Posterior Chain
1. **Single-Leg Glute Bridge with Weight**
   - 4x8 each leg
   - Weight on bent leg (start 10lb, progress to 25lb)
   - 2-sec hold at top

2. **Calf Raises**
   - 3xAMRAP (as many reps as possible)
   - Goal: 30 each leg by Week 9
   - Single-leg, bodyweight to start
   - Progress to weighted when achieving 3x30

### Knee & ITB Protection
1. **Wall Sit**
   - 3x30sec
   - Progress to 3x45sec by Week 5
   - Progress to 3x60sec by Week 9

2. **Step Downs**
   - 3x15 each leg
   - 6-8" step height
   - Slow, controlled eccentric (3-sec lower)
   - Focus on knee tracking over toes

# PHASE 2: DURABILITY (Weeks 10-20)

## Overview
**Duration:** 11 weeks (April 6 - June 21, 2026)  
**Volume:** Build 20mpw → 40mpw (Weeks 10-12), then hold 40-50mpw  
**Vert:** Progressive 4k → 9k/week  
**Structure:** 3 training blocks

### Block 1 (Weeks 10-13): Steady State
**Focus:** Continue volume build + aerobic endurance  
**Key Workouts:** 2x15min, 2x20min at steady state pace (9:30-10:00/mi)  
**Deload:** Week 13

### Block 2 (Weeks 14-17): Tempo + Hills
**Focus:** Lactate threshold + power hiking  
**Key Workouts:** 3x8min tempo, 4x3min hill repeats  
**Deload:** Week 17

### Block 3 (Weeks 18-20): VO2 Max
**Focus:** Running economy + continuous uphill  
**Key Workouts:** 5x3min at 5k effort, 500-1000ft continuous climbs  
**Deload:** Week 20 (final prep for Specificity)

### Strength Transition
**Weeks 10-20:** Heavy 2x/week + Runner Maintenance 3x/week
- **Tuesday/Thursday post-run:** Heavy lifts (Dana's program)
- **Mon/Wed/Fri:** Runner maintenance (10-15min: calf raises, SL glute bridge, planks, hip flexor stretches)
- **No more daily PT** - You've built the foundation

### Rowing Integration
**Monday:** 30min Z2 (active recovery)  
**Friday:** Easy run OR rowing (based on feel)  
**Saturday:** 40-50min steady state rowing (aerobic boost)
